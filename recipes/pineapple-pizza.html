Pineapple Pizza

A questionable pineapple pizza from Old School Runescape. This item of food heals your player character for 22 hitpoints in two bites and requires 45 cooking.

This item of food requires the following ingredients:

  • Pineapple
  • Bucket of Water
  • Pot of Flour
  • Cheese
  • Tomato

The following items are also required:

  • Knife

A pineaplle pizza can be made by following these steps:

  1. Combine a pot of flour with a bucket of water to create a pizza base.
  2. Use tomato on the pizza base to create an incomplete pizza.
  3. Use cheese on an incomplete pizza to create an uncooked pizza.
  4. Cook an uncooked pizza on a range to create a plain pizza.
  5. Use a knife on a pineapple to create pineapple chunks or pineapple rings
    • Note: If you choose to create pineapple rings, you can create a total of 4 pizzas, versus just one pizza if you were to create pineapple chunks in the previous step.
