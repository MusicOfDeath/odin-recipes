Summer Pie

A tasty summer pie from Old School Runescape. This item of food heals your player character for 22 hitpoints in two bites and requires 95 cooking. A summer pie will boost the player's Agility level by +5 temporarily as well as restoring 10% run energy.

This item of food requires the following ingredients:

  • Cooking Apple
  • Watermelon
  • Strawberry
  • Pot of Flour
  • Bucket of Water

The following items are also required:

  • Pie Dish

A summer pie can be made by following these steps:

  1. Combine a pot of flour with a bucket of water to obtain pastry dough.
  2. Combine pastry dough with the pie dish to create a pie shell.
  3. Use a strawberry on the pie shell to make a part summer pie (strawberry).
  4. Use a watermelon on the part summer pie (strawberry) to make a part summer pie (watermelon).
  5. Use a cooking apple on the part summer pie (watermelon) to make a raw summer pie.
  6. Cook the raw summer pie on a range to finish a summer pie.
